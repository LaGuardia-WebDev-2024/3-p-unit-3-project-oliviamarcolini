ARROZ À PIAMONTESE

Ingredients Quantity
rice 2 cups
oil 1 tablespoon
minced garlic 1 clove
salt 1 tablespoon
hot water 4 cups
cream 300 grams
grated parmesan cheese 100 grams
mushroom 1 cup

Step 1: In a pan, place 1 tablespoon of oil, 1 clove of chopped garlic, 2 cups of rice and sauté for 1 minute.

Step 2: Add 1 tablespoon of salt, 4 cups of hot water and mix. Let it cook until there is little water left.

Step 3: Add 300 grams of cream, 100 grams of grated parmesan cheese and 1 cup of mushrooms. Mix until all the cheese has melted.

BRIGADEIRO

Ingredients Quantity
butter 1 tablespoon
condensed milk 1 can
chocolate powder 30 grams
chocolate sprinkles
(I like it better with
granulated sugar) 		150 grams

Step 1: In a pan, mix the condensed milk, cocoa powder and butter. Turn on the heat and stir until it comes away from the bottom of the pan.

Step 2: Wait for it to cool, grease your hands with butter and shape them into spheres.

Step 3: Roll the brigadeiros in sprinkles (or granulated sugar), place them in paper liners and serve.

PASTEL

Dough Ingredients Quantity
wheat flour 480 grams
oil 2 tablespoons
warm water 1 cup
salt 2 teaspoons
cachaça 2 tablespoons
Filling
(You can choose 1,
or multiple!)
ground beef sautéed to taste 250 grams
chopped mozzarela and ham 250 grams
nutella and chopped strawberries 250 grams

Step 1: In a bowl, place 3 cups of wheat flour, 2 tablespoons of oil, 1 cup of warm water, 2 teaspoons of salt, 2 tablespoons of cachaça and knead well until a homogeneous dough forms.

Step 2: Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes or until the dough is smooth.

Step 3: Roll the dough through the roller until it is very thin.

Step 4: Using a knife, cut rectangles or discs of the size you prefer, place the filling in the middle and fold the dough to form the pastry.

Step 5: Using a fork, press the edges to seal well.

Step 6: Fry the pastries in hot oil until golden brown. Remove and drain on absorbent paper.

Step 7: All done, serve and enjoy!

BOLINHO DE BACALHAU

Ingredients Quantity
olive oil 1 tablespoon
garlic half a tablespoon
chopped onion 2 tablespoons
medium boiled potatoes
(peeled and mashed) 		2
beaten egg 1
cooked and shredded cod 200 grams
breadcrumbs 30 grams
olive oil 1 tablespoon
chopped parsley to taste
ground black pepper to taste
salt to taste

Step 1: In a pan with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, sauté half a tablespoon of garlic and 2 tablespoons of chopped onion until golden.

Step 2: Transfer the sautéed mixture to a bowl.

Step 3: Add 2 medium cooked and mashed potatoes, 1 beaten egg, 200 grams of cooked and shredded cod, a quarter of a cup of breadcrumbs, 1 tablespoon of olive oil, chopped parsley, ground black pepper, salt to taste.

Step 4: Mix well until a homogeneous dough forms. Take small portions of dough, make balls and fry in hot oil until golden brown.

Step 5: Remove, drain on absorbent paper and serve!

COXINHA

Filling Ingredients Quantity
chicken broth tablets 1
chicken breast 1
onion to taste
garlic to taste
parsley to taste
chives to taste
rosemary to taste
Dough
water 2 cups
margarine 1 tablespoon
wheat flour 2 cups
Assembly
beaten eggs 2
breadcrumbs 1 cup

Filling

Step 1: In a saucepan, add enough water to cook the chicken. Add 2 chicken broth cubes and 1 chicken breast.

Step 2: Cook until the chicken breast is tender.

Step 3: Use two forks to shred the chicken breast.

Step 4: Place the shredded chicken breast in the pan and sauté with onion and garlic to taste.

Step 5: Season the shredded chicken with parsley, chives and rosemary. Set aside.

Dough

Step 1: In a saucepan, place 2 cups of water from the cooking/preparation of the chicken and 1 tablespoon of margarine. Let it boil

Step 2: Add 2 cups of wheat flour. Mix quickly to avoid lumps.

Step 3: Let it cool and set aside.

Assembly

Step 1: Take the dough and make balls.

Step 2: Open the dough in your hand and add the filling.

Step 3: Close the dough and shape it into a coxinha.

Step 4: Coat the coxinha in the beaten egg and breadcrumbs.

Step 5: Fry in hot oil. Using a slotted spoon, remove the coxinha and drain until dry.
